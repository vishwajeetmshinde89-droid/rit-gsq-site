Program Overview

A Structured Platform for Academic Excellence

RIT Global Scholar Quest (GSQ) is a first-of-its-kind initiative designed to systematically identify, evaluate, and mentor high-potential students across leading international schools in India. It bridges school-level academic excellence with global undergraduate innovation pathways.
